About



This website was created in 2008 and forgotten about since last year. Ever since some friends found it, it's become a place for my friends to find old pictures of me without asking me, which has proven to be a bit odd over time. Since then, I proposed the idea of reforming the site to my parents, which they would now like me to do. Here is the final product.


All of the photos and videos shown on this website are from the old one, so this is going to be targeted as a nostalgic-based website.
